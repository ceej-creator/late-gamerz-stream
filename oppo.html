Based on the viewer’s feedback we might need to have further optimizations on our live and smooth internet connection so this will be a crucial part for our area of improvement. This will be in our area of improvement by fixing the quality and the perfect position of the camera since this is crucial for our live streaming. Some of the viewers suggested that we might need to improve such things as, they can see all of us, not just 1 or 2 people, Improving the gaming vibe of the players, more involvement, and extended time frame. By this, we will further improve our way of fulfilling the viewer’s request bringing a much lively and adaptable livestream and considering the obstacles preventing it.
