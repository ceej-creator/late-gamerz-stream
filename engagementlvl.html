LEVEL OF ENGAGEMENT

The primary objective of our live stream was to provide entertainment through gaming,
and our chosen game for our first live stream was Mobile Legends. The effectiveness
of our choice of game and platform for the stream was reflected in our viewership.
Garnering more than 290 views for the first stream shows that this had a positive
effect on our viewership and attracted our target audience.

Being conscious of our target audience also affected engagement, making it easier to know how to
interact with our viewers, and in turn, our live stream achieved a medium level of engagement through
friendly and humorous interactions with viewers. The key strategy involved direct interaction with
the participants by responding to their chats, giving shout-outs, answering questions, and
implementing viewer-suggested challenges into the gameplay, fostering a sense of collaboration
and investment.

Chat activity and viewership numbers were the main indicators of engagement, with noteworthy
interactions contributing to the stream's lighthearted atmosphere. While not reaching peak engagement
levels, participants expressed enjoyment of the experience through the chat box while streaming,
and our feedback survey after the live took place, indicating a successful, albeit moderately
engaging live stream.
