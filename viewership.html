VIEWERSHIP


Our first stream had 292 total views, which shows that we had an extremely
high audience rate right from the start. Even after facing technical difficulties
that forced us to restart the stream, our second stream still pulled in an impressive
207 views, and our third reached 166 views, proving that our audience remained highly engaged.

In terms of engagement, our first stream had 2 shares, while the second had 3 shares, and
the last had 5 shares, showing that people were actively sharing our Livestream. Our average
concurrent viewers were around 6 to 7, with a peak of about 17 to 19 viewers, which is
a very strong viewership rate for our streams. We kept our audience hooked by answering
their questions, giving shoutouts, and interacting through speech and the chat box.

Our viewership performance has been outstanding, and if we continue to engage with our audience
and possibly expand our content by playing more games such as Valorant, Marvel Rivals, CS:GO 2,
League of Legends, Minecraft and etc, we can attract even more viewers and continue
building on this success.
